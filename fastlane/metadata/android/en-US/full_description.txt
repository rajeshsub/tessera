Tessera is a free, open-source two-factor authentication app for Android. It generates TOTP
and HOTP codes entirely on your device.

Privacy first:
* No accounts, no servers, no analytics, no ads.
* No unnecessary permissions.
* Your secrets are stored in a single vault, encrypted with a key only you hold.

Yours to keep:
* Encrypted backup to storage you choose.
* A gated plaintext export using standard otpauth URIs, so you can move to any other
  authenticator at any time.

Simple and fast:
* Add accounts by scanning a QR code, importing an image, or entering details by hand.
* Import from Google Authenticator and standard otpauth links.
* Optional biometric unlock, with your passphrase always available as a fallback.

The F-Droid build contains zero Google Play Services. A separate Play Store build adds a Wear
OS companion that displays codes computed by your phone.
